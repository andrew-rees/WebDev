"The Cup of the World"

What is the Cup, of the World?
Is it the place where we all keep our drinks?
Is it the object that 'cups'? And if it is,
What does it cup?
When you put something in it, does it taste funny?
When you hide it in a cave, does Harrison Ford try to find it?
Would you think it was odd to put water in it? (Does water only go in glasses?).
I think it's gold, but it might be spraypainted.
I wonder if the bottom is felt, or cork?
The Cup of the World, it holds all our liquids.
