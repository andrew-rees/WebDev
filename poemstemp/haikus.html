A Haiku is a Japanese poem or something like that. It's short because the Japanese don't screw about with long sentences, because they just get stuff done

Trees make tables

Tree stands by building. tree?
Where are it's legs?
How long will you stand, my fine

Cross

The signpost looked just
How it should; why not point to
Many places at once?

Do you

Do you rest on grass
On a cool morning? Do you turn
to vapour now, dew?
